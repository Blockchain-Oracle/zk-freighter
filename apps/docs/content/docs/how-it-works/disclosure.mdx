Privacy products live or die on compliance. ZK Freighter's answer is **user-held selective
disclosure**: you can prove facts about your own shielded funds to someone you choose, without
giving them any power over the funds.

## How it works

The Disclosure screen produces a **read-only proof of a specific note** — its existence and your
ownership — that a counterparty or auditor can verify. Two powers stay deliberately separated:

- **Spend authority** — never leaves your wallet
- **View evidence** — the disclosure receipt, which you hand over explicitly

Proving runs on your device; nothing is uploaded. The receipt covers exactly the note you chose —
not your balance, not your other notes, not your history.

## Verifying a receipt

The same screen verifies receipts you receive from others, so both sides of an audit can run it
without trusting a server.

## Why this design

The alternative — a custodial relayer that logs identities for compliance — puts a middleman in
every payment who can see everything. ZK Freighter's association-set gate (screening at the pool
door) plus user-held disclosure (proof on demand) achieves compliance without anyone holding a
master view of your activity.
