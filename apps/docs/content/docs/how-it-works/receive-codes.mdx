---
title: Private receive codes
description: The zkf1… code that lets anyone pay your shielded balance without knowing your address.
---

To be paid privately, you share a **private receive code** — a `zkf1…` string (Bech32m encoding,
human-readable prefix `zkf`) shown as text and QR in every surface's Receive screen.

## What's inside

The code packs two public keys derived from your seed:

- your **note public key** — ownership of the notes a sender creates for you
- your **encryption public key** — lets the sender encrypt the note details so only you can
  discover them on-chain

It contains **no spending power and no link to your Stellar address**. Sharing it is safe; it
only lets people pay you privately.

## Discovery

Your wallet finds incoming notes by scanning the pool's encrypted outputs with your keys —
locally. Nobody else can tell which notes are yours.

## Optional: make your code discoverable

If you want people to find your code from your public Stellar address, you can opt in to
publishing the link on-chain ("Make my code discoverable" in the Receive screen). This is a
**public, deliberate act** — it never exposes your seed or any power to spend, only the mapping
from address to receive code. Skip it and share the code out-of-band for maximum privacy.

## Recovery

Codes are deterministic: restoring your wallet from the 12-word phrase regenerates the same
receive code on any device.
