ZK Fighter uses **Circle's CCTP** (Cross-Chain Transfer Protocol) to bring native USDC onto
Stellar. CCTP burns USDC on the source chain and mints genuine USDC on Stellar — no wrapped
IOU. Supported sources: **Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism** (Sepolia testnets are the
demo surface).

## The burn is signed by your own wallet

The EVM-side burn is signed with an **EVM key derived from your same seed phrase** — no
MetaMask, no WalletConnect, no separate wallet to manage.

## Two honest steps, not one fake atomic

Circle's contract mints USDC to the recipient — it does not call the privacy pool. So the flow
is deliberately two labeled steps:

1. **Bridge arrival (public):** burn → Circle attestation (this can take minutes; the UI shows
   real progress) → Stellar mint. Every leg has a real transaction hash.
2. **Shield (public boundary):** a separate deposit moves the arrived USDC into the pool.

An atomic bridge-and-shield would require a custom adapter contract; it stays out of the product
until it exists and passes real tests. We do not fake it.
